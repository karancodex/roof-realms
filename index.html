The Roof & Realms 12-Step Modular Journey

From concept to handover – precision engineered, meticulously executed

🏠
1

Project Definition & Goals

Understand your vision, site requirements, budget constraints, and project objectives through detailed consultation.

2

Design Development & Feasibility

Create preliminary designs, conduct site analysis, and establish project feasibility with technical assessments.

3

Engineering & Modular Planning

Detailed BIM modeling, structural engineering, modular breakdown, and complete technical documentation.

4

Foundation & Site Preparation

Precision groundwork, foundation installation, and site infrastructure setup parallel to factory fabrication.

5

Factory Fabrication (LGSF Frames, Walls, Roofs)

CNC-controlled fabrication of steel frames, wall panels, roof trusses, and modular components in controlled factory environment.

6

Module Inspection & Quality Control

Multi-stage quality checks, dimensional verification, material testing, and compliance certification at factory.

7

Transportation & Logistics

Carefully planned transportation of prefabricated modules to site with specialized handling and protection.

8

On-Site Module Assembly & Connection

Rapid assembly of modules on prepared foundation, structural connections, and weatherproofing integration.

9

Systems Integration (MEP)

Installation of mechanical, electrical, and plumbing systems. HVAC, fire safety, and smart building integration.

10

Finishing, Site Works & Landscaping

Interior finishes, exterior cladding, landscaping, paving, and all aesthetic completion work.

11

Testing, Commissioning & Handover

Comprehensive systems testing, compliance verification, documentation, final walkthrough, and official handover.

12

Post-Handover Support & Lifecycle Partnership

12-month defects liability, maintenance support, warranty management, and long-term partnership for future needs.
