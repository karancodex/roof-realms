About Roof & Realms

Engineering certainty in how India builds

We Engineer Certainty in How India Builds

Roof & Realms exists to solve one problem: traditional construction in India is unpredictable. Timelines slip. Costs escalate. Quality varies.

We took a different approach. By combining Light Gauge Steel Frame (LGSF) technology, factory-controlled precision and sector-specific expertise, we've transformed construction from a chaotic, on-site experiment into a managed, predictable product.

Today, we partner with home buyers, developers, corporates and institutions across residential, commercial, hospitality, healthcare, education and industrial sectors. Each project proves the same truth: when you standardize what should be standard and customize what should be unique, uncertainty disappears.

Our mission: Make modular construction India's first choice for speed, quality and certainty.
